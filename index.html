Coloring Journal

A place to color, write + paste

The surging popularity of grown-up coloring books joined forces with Trina’s natural instinct to create, and the results are shown in every page of Color Write + Paste. A vellum pocket attached inside the front cover holds handpicked ephemera, perfect inspiration for both long-time mixed-media lovers and those just discovering the medium. This 40 page book is the place to capture your memories and celebrate your imagination. “I invite you to color, write and paste with me.”
